Package Dimensions

  • Case goods are packaged in boxes and come partially- or fully-assembled. Please check the product details for assembly time estimates.
  • Upholstery items are wrapped in a protective plastic packaging for transit.
  • For safe and secure transit, always verify that you have extra room in addition to the package dimensions listed.

Store Pickup

  • Free pickup is available at any store location or distribution center every day from 12pm to 9pm.
  • Same-day pickup is available at our distribution centers in Rialto and Freemont, and at our Phoenix store.
  • Next-day pickup is available at every store except our Summerlin location, where you can pick up items 2 days after placing your order.
